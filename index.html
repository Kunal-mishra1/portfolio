About Kunal Mishra

Senior Product Leader & VP of Product Aspirant | AI, Robotics & Fintech Expert

In the heart of San Francisco, my journey in product management began at Amazon Robotics, where I defined the initial product roadmap for an AI-powered warehouse robot. Through rapid MVP sprints and design thinking workshops, I validated assumptions, iterated on real user insights, and improved throughput by 25%.

At E*TRADE, I led extensive market research and deep user research sessions to uncover trader pain points. This drove our gamification MVP, which boosted new-account sign-ups by 40% and aligned the team around clear OKRs for engagement and retention.

My tenure at OTPP was defined by a mission to engineer a predictive margin calculator and scenario-test a $230 billion capital-markets book. I crafted a robust product strategy, coordinated cross-functional teams on a unified roadmap, and maintained a customer-centric approach to elevate risk-model precision.

  • Product Strategy & Roadmaps: Aligning stakeholders with quarterly OKRs and measurable milestones.
  • MVP Development: Rapid prototyping, A/B testing, and user validation to de-risk features.
  • User & Market Research: Data-driven insights powering every design-thinking session.
  • Customer-Centric Innovation: Empathy-led solutions that translate into real business impact.

My philosophy blends experimentation-led development with a relentless focus on the customer. From strategy and roadmaps to MVPs and OKRs, I mentor cross-functional teams to deliver scalable, high-impact products. Let’s connect and explore how I can drive innovation for your organization.
